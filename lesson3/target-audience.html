Target Audience

The target audience of the weathergo.io website is described below:

  • Who: Those who are interested in learning more about weather forecasting.
  • Age: 15-80
  • Income: $0K-$120K
  • Education: High School and higher
  • Experience with the Topic: Everyday Use
  • Family Status: Single, Married
  • Where, When and How the Visitor Will Be Accessing the Site: Early mornings, and at the end of the work day.
